About Dowell Logistics

The Brokerage Arm of Dowell Express

Dowell Logistics is the freight brokerage division of Dowell Express, a trusted asset-based trucking operation headquartered in Jefferson, GA. Together, we offer shippers something most brokers simply can't — guaranteed access to real assets when capacity tightens.

We leverage our own fleet alongside a vetted network of carrier partners to ensure your freight moves on time, every time — no matter the market conditions.

Asset-Backed Advantage Access to Dowell Express trucks gives you priority capacity that independent brokers can't offer.
Carrier Relationships Years of partnerships with vetted, reliable carriers across North America.
Transparent Communication Real-time updates and dedicated support from dispatch to delivery.
